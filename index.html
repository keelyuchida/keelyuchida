About

I make brands feel like something.

I started as a dancer — trained at CalArts, performed on commercial sets and editorial shoots, and learned early that the most powerful communication happens when something moves you. That instinct carried straight into my marketing work.

At CalArts, I built campaigns that drove real enrollment growth, managed donor-funded initiatives in the millions, and led a content team from the ground up. On the side, I've directed movement for Amazon, appeared in international editorial, and collaborated with artists you know.

I'm a strategist who can execute and a creative who can back it up with data. That's a rare combination — and exactly what I bring to every room I'm in.
